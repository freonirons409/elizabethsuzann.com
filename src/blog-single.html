SS16: NARROWS OF THE HARPETH

July 21, 2016

Our Spring/Summer Collection this year was designed for the heat, for the outdoors, for the sticky, humid days in the middle of summer where the sun is high and you don’t want anything touching your skin. We had the opportunity to capture that still, sweet, feeling right here in Nashville. We traveled up to the Narrows of the Harpeth, expecting it to be a sunny, hot summer day. It was over 90 degrees by 9AM. We embraced the harsh, mid-day light, the beads of sweat on our models brow. This shoot, perhaps more than most, was a true representation of the inspiration behind and functionality of a collection. The natural cotton, hemp, and silk fabrics are meant to absorb moisture. The silhouettes are cut to stand away from the body and let air flow across your skin - all illustrated on a woman we are inspired by and call a friend.

These images are beautiful in their own right - when shooting with Zachary that’s always the case - but I am even more fulfilled by the resolution this shoot brings to this collection. It was inspired by shapes, the outdoors, traveling, heat, and over the past several months we’ve been able to visually document every facet and explore those concepts. This season, I’m not only proud of the collection itself, but I’m proud of the way we’ve brought it to life, and of the way you have each picked up on the story and made these pieces your own...

"I've been wearing [these pieces] on a weekly basis since they arrived. I can honestly say Elizabeth Suzann is worth the wait if you're serious about responsible fashion and true craftsmanship." - Alex

Chelsea wears the Harlow Dress in Ivory Hemp Gauze and Moss Silk Crepe, the Diane Trench in Natural Cotton Twill and Black Cotton Twill, the Elodie Work Belt in Natural Cotton Twill and Black Silk Crepe, the Inez Midi in Ivory Hemp Twill and Black Silk Crepe, Darby Work Dress in Ivory Hemp Twill, the Keaton Duster in Natural Cotton Twill, the Harlow Tunic in Black Hemp Gauze and Black Silk Crepe, the Bel Skirt in Natural Handwoven Cotton and Black Cotton Voile, the Birdie Crop in Natural Handwoven Cotton, the Petra Crop in Black Cotton Voile, the Inez Tunic in Moss Silk Crepe, the Birdie Caftan in Black Handwoven Cotton, the Inez Crop in Flax Hemp Twill, and the Mule in Graphite and Sandstone.

OS-, OS, OS+ is our new method of sizing previously One Sized garments. If you ordered a One Size garment from us before, the OS will fit exactly the same way - there are just added options now for smaller and larger frames. OS fits most comfortably, but we offer OS- for more petite frames. This size is recommended for those 5' 3" and below, those who have narrow shoulders, or those who just prefer a slimmer fit. OS+ is best for sizes 12 and above, those with broader shoulders, or those who prefer a more oversized fit.

 

What size should I order?

How to take your measurements: Measure carefully with a tape measure, fitting the tape snugly (but not tightly) to your body. Order based on your actual measurements — do not size up or down unless noted in the product description, or you desire a fit different than depicted on the model. Most of our garments are intended to have a relaxed, comfortable fit. This is the way they are designed - for ease of wear and to increase the longevity of the garment by avoiding added stress on the seams.

The graphic below is a visual summary of how to find your size in various ES garments. Just find the measurements closest to your own body, and work to the left!

If you are unsure about the sizing of a specific garment, our support team would be happy to help, so please send us an email before ordering!
