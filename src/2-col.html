What size should I order?

How to take your measurements: Measure carefully with a tape measure, fitting the tape snugly (but not tightly) to your body. Order based on your actual measurements â€” do not size up or down unless noted in the product description, or you desire a fit different than depicted on the model. Most of our garments are intended to have a relaxed, comfortable fit. This is the way they are designed - for ease of wear and to increase the longevity of the garment by avoiding added stress on the seams.

The graphic below is a visual summary of how to find your size in various ES garments. Just find the measurements closest to your own body, and work to the left!

If you are unsure about the sizing of a specific garment, our support team would be happy to help, so please send us an email before ordering!
