  • Details

    The Linn Tee is one of our simplest pieces, and sometimes simple is best. Structural cut lets the fabric do the heavy lifting - the beautiful folds and swing look right at home with pin-straight pants at a gallery opening, or over a gauzy dress on a sticky weekend afternoon.

    Cut from a sturdy, structured, incredibly durable linen that can be pressed to a neat, crisp finish, or left rumpled for a perfectly lived-in look. It has luxurious body and weight, and the strength to stand up to years of daily wear. It will only soften and improve with age, a beautiful benefit of clothing meant for a life fully-lived.

    This top has an easy balance of comfort and interesting silhouette. Elbow length kimono sleeves aren't bulky or constricting, the high neckline shows just the right amount of collarbone, and the cropped length looks great with our high waisted trousers or layered over a dress. Trapeze cut creates one of my favorite shapes and is incredibly easy to move around in.

  • Fit & Material

    Intended Fit

    Intended to have a relaxed, oversized fit. OS fits most, but we offer OS- for smaller-framed, petite customers, and OS+ for larger-framed, broader folks. The OS- is recommended for those 5'3" and shorter, or those who prefer a slimmer, narrower fit. OS+ is recommended for sizes 12 and above, or those who have broader shoulders or prefer a more oversized fit. See our size chart for more details on selecting the correct size for your fit preference.
