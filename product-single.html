  • Details

    The Petra Crop is a reincarnation of one of our earliest styles (The Lee Crop, a favorite from several Springs ago). A simple, classic crop top meant for summertime. The textural fabric and slim fitting silhouette make this the perfect topper for high-waisted skirts, your favorite pair of old jeans, or straight-leg trousers. Weekend getaways to Cape Cod - wear this paired with the matching Nadeen for afternoon oceanside walks or over a soft, flowing skirt for dinner and drinks.

    Cut from a sturdy, durable but soft hemp twill that can be pressed to a neat, crisp finish, or left rumpled for a perfectly lived-in look. The perfect complement to our Signature linen fabric - it has luxurious body and the undefinable quality of possessing weight and lightness simultaneously. Textured with a beautiful grid-like pattern, almost like a dobby cloth. It will only soften and improve with age, a beautiful benefit of clothing meant for a life fully-lived.

    Simple, easy silhouette. High, boat neck, slim-fitting in the chest with bust darts, stands away from the body at the waist. Generous 2" hem provides structure and balance. Carefully cut armholes hit right at the most flattering part of the shoulder - shows everything you want to show and nothing you don't. Cropped length hits at the natural waist and pairs perfectly with high-rise pants and skirts, layers well over slip-dresses.
