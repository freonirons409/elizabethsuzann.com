  • Details

    The Cecilia Pant, named after that heartbreaking muse of Paul Simon's, is one of those classics that doesn't need much explaining. A high-rise, slim fitting cigarette silhouette that wears just as well under chunky knits as silk tanks.

    Stretch denim is sturdy, form-fitting, and comfortable - these are pants you can run, sit on the floor, live in. Pack these (they won't wrinkle!) for your weekend trips away - they'll easily get you from daytime sight-seeing to late night cocktails with a change of blouse and shoes. Pants like this make getting dressed simple. Wear with literally anything - a linen Georgia Tee for the most fuss-free oufit imaginable, a silk Artist Smock knotted at the waist for an elegant, hourglass silhouette, a Marlena Midi for a more avant garde look.

    - High-rise, faced waistline
    - Ankle length w/ wide hem
    - Center back zipper
    - Sturdy, wrinkle resistant fabric
    - 97% cotton, 3% spandex
    - Available in 00-16 (Short, Regular, Tall)
    - Cut and sewn to order just for you in our Nashville studio

    OS-, OS, OS+ is our new method of sizing previously One Sized garments. If you ordered a One Size garment from us before, the OS will fit exactly the same way - there are just added options now for smaller and larger frames. OS+ is best for sizes 12 and above, those with broader shoulders, or those who prefer a more oversized fit.
