  • Details

    Every woman knows there's something indescribably perfect about throwing on a man's oversized button down shirt. The loose comfort, casually rolled sleeve, the soft, high quality fabric, the way the curved hem highlights just the right part of your leg. I've been intrigued at length by this classic garment, and the Annie Shirtdress is my conceptual interpretation of it. Very pared down with no buttons, plackets, or collar - a high jewel neck to make the silhouette a little more interesting, generous dropped sleeves for that perfect oversized look, back yolk and center front and back seams to elongate the body, and topstitching on all seams for durability. The shaped shirttail hem hits at the perfect shirtdress length - short enough to wear easily over pants, but long enough to comfortably wear as a dress.

    Cut from a lightweight, thin - but strong - hemp/silk/cotton blend. Floats and drapes like air, but has beautiful body and volume. Semi-sheer with a dynamic horizontal slub and bubbly, organic texture. An extremely comfortable and easy to wear fabric.

    You can wear this dress quite literally anywhere - lounging in the apartment on Sunday mornings, padding around with bare legs and feet to grab coffee and the newspaper, over torn up denim and birks with the Elodie Work Belt holding your pencils while you sketch outdoors, or over pin-straight black trousers to a gallery opening. True comfort and ease - so light and airy you'll forget what you're wearing.

    - Simple shirtdress
    - Long sleeves, topstitched seam
    - Hits just past the knee
    - 42% hemp, 35% silk, 23% organic cotton gauze
    - Available in OS-, OS, and OS+
    - Cut and sewn to order just for you in our Nashville studio

  • Fit & Material

    Intended Fit

    Intended to have a relaxed, oversized fit. OS fits most, but we offer OS- for smaller-framed, petite customers, and OS+ for larger-framed, broader folks. The OS- is recommended for those 5'3" and shorter, or those who prefer a slimmer, narrower fit. OS+ is recommended for sizes 12 and above, or those who have broader shoulders or prefer a more oversized fit. See our size chart for more details on selecting the correct size for your fit preference.

    Model Specs

    Renee is 5’ 9.5” tall, and has a 33” bust, 25” waist, and 35” hips. She is wearing the OS.

    Material

    42% Hemp, 35% Silk, 23% Organic Cotton. Soft, lightweight, and sheer with a visible horizontal slub and lots of texture.

    Two classic colors:
    - Soft black
    - Warm ivory

    Care

    Hand or machine wash on gentle cycle with cold water. Tumble dry low. Leave unpressed for a beautifully rumpled look, or press on medium heat with steam to remove wrinkles, straighten neckline, sleeves and hem. Will press to a crisp, smooth finish.

What size should I order?

How to take your measurements: Measure carefully with a tape measure, fitting the tape snugly (but not tightly) to your body. Order based on your actual measurements — do not size up or down unless noted in the product description, or you desire a fit different than depicted on the model. Most of our garments are intended to have a relaxed, comfortable fit. This is the way they are designed - for ease of wear and to increase the longevity of the garment by avoiding added stress on the seams.

The graphic below is a visual summary of how to find your size in various ES garments. Just find the measurements closest to your own body, and work to the left!

If you are unsure about the sizing of a specific garment, our support team would be happy to help, so please send us an email before ordering!
